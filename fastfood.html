what are the most popular fast food chains in the us?

the most popular fast food chains in the us are Starbucks, subway, dunkin, Burger King, chipotle, Wendy's, Taco Bell, chic fil a, and McDonalds

One of the most famous restaurants in the United States is McDonald's.It started in 1955 and became popular because it was a place that served food quickly. It's also cheap for families and people traveling on the road. McDonald’s made fast food popular with foods like hamburgers, fries, and milkshakes. Today it has lots of locations all over and is one of the most well known restaurants in the world.

Another popular restaurant is Subway.Subway began in 1965 when a college student named Fred DeLuca opened a small sandwich shop to help pay for his school. The restaurant became famous for making sandwiches filled with meat, cheese, and vegetables. Over time, Subway grew into one of the biggest restaurants in the United States, with thousands of locations where people can get quick sandwiches and salads.

Another popular restaurant in the United States is Chick‑fil‑A. This restaurant is known for its chicken sandwiches.Many people enjoy eating there because the food is simple and tastes good. Even though it has fewer locations than some other restaurants, Chick fil A makes a lot of sales and has become one of the most popular fast food restaurants.
